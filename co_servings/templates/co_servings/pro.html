High level description of the functions in the smart contracts


deposit():   Allows the investor to deposit the required loan amount to the smart contract. The deposited amount is then used to borrow funds from the Venus Protocol.


borrow():   Allows the owners to borrow funds from the Venus Protocol, using the deposited amount as collateral.


repayLoan():   Allows the borrower to repay the borrowed funds to the Venus Protocol.


calculateProfit():   Calculates the profit earned from the project once it is completed. The profit is then divided among the owners and the borrower.


distributeProfit():   Distributes the profit earned from the project among the owners and the borrower according to the specified percentage shares.


transferOwnership():   Transfers the ownership of the project to the specified address.


Modifiers onlyOwners and onlyBorrower:   These modifiers ensure that only authorized owners and the borrower can execute specific functions, preventing unauthorized access to the smart contract.
