How does'it work:
Step 1:  Fill Up the Supply Section in Venus Protocol(VP)
VP is a decentralized money market protocol on the Binance Smart Chain that enables users to lend and borrow various cryptocurrencies, including stablecoins, using collateralized loans. InvestorA supplies assets to VP to earn interest on those assets. VP offers variable interest rates on supplied assets that are determined by the supply and demand for those assets on the protocol. To create Passive income: Supplying assets to VP can be a way to generate passive income without having to actively trade or invest in other assets. VP is built on the Binance Smart Chain, a highly secure and decentralized blockchain, where assets "supply" are held in a secure and transparent manner. VP is designed to minimize risk for users by offering over-collateralized loans and a liquidation system that ensures that loans are fully backed at all time by collateral.
Step 2:   InvestorB provides assets to the Borrow Section in VP
When the VP's Supply Section reaches the value of $15M in BNB coin, InvestorB adds $10M to InvestorA'Wallet to be deposited in the Borrow Section of VP.    InvestorB gains 6% return on the borrowed capital by BorrowerC.   InvestorB takes 6 percent return on the loaned capital, but does not share in the profits of the project because his investment is guaranteed by the funds available in InvestorA's Wallet.
Step 3:   BorrowerC takes a $10M loan at 6% interest from Borrow Section in VP
BorrowerC selects a project type from the Catalogue and develops ProjectD. When ProjectD is finished he returns Loan+Interest to VP's Borrow Section. InvestorB can then take back his Investment+Interest from InvestorA's Wallet. BorrowerC keeps 40% of profit and distributes 40% to InvestorA and 20% to the Co-Serving platform which managed and coded all transactions. The property of ProjectD remains in InvestorA's Wallet untill all the smart contracts conditions are sucessfully resolved.
