Design-Build Method:

"A design-build company is responsible for managing a building project from the initial design stage to the final construction stage. Here are the main tasks that a design-build company includes when making a design-build building:"
How does'it work:
Step 1:  Pre-Construction Services:
The design-build company will work with the Borrower-C to establish the project goals, budget, and schedule. They will also determine the site, project, feasibility, secure permits and approvals, and perform environmental assessments.
Step 2:   Design Phase:
The design-build company will develop the design for the project based on the Borrower-C's needs, budget, and schedule. This includes architectural, structural, mechanical, electrical, and plumbing designs. They will also work with the client to choose materials and finishes.
Step 3:   Value Engineering:
The design-build company will evaluate the design to ensure it meets the Borrower-C's requirements and budget. They will suggest cost-saving measures that don't compromise the quality or functionality of the project.
Step 4:   Construction Phase:
The design-build company will manage the construction of the project. They will hire subcontractors, manage the construction schedule, and ensure the project is built according to the design and specifications.
Step 5:   Quality Control:
The design-build company will ensure the quality of the project by monitoring the construction process, conducting regular inspections, and addressing any issues that arise.
Step 6:   Project Closeout:
The design-build company will ensure that the project is completed on time, within budget, and to the client's satisfaction. They will also handle the final inspections, obtain final permits, and provide the client with all necessary documentation.
