This is how it works:
Step 1:  Fill Up the Supply Section in Venus Protocol(VP)
VP is a decentralized money market protocol on the Binance Smart Chain that enables users to lend and borrow various cryptocurrencies, including stablecoins, using collateralized loans. InvestorA supplies assets to VP to earn interest on those assets. VP offers variable interest rates on supplied assets that are determined by the supply and demand for those assets on the protocol. To create Passive income: Supplying assets to VP can be a way to generate passive income without having to actively trade or invest in other assets. VP is built on the Binance Smart Chain, which is a highly secure and decentralized blockchain. This means that assets supplied to Venus Protocol are held in a secure and transparent manner.Venus Protocol is designed to minimize risk for users by offering over-collateralized loans and a liquidation system that ensures that loans are fully backed by collateral at all times.   To then share with others investors who provided supply assets 40% of the project's profit.
Step 2:   InvestorB provide assets to the Borrow Section in VP
When the Supply Section of VP reaches the value of $15M in BNB coin, InvestorB adds $10M in InvestorA wallet to be deposited in the Borrow Section of VP.    InvestorB takes 6% return on the borrowed capital by BorrowerC.   InvestorB takes 6 percent return on the loaned capital, but does not share profits of the project because his investment is guaranteed by the funds available in InvestarA's portfolio.
Step 3:   BorrowerC takes $10M loan at 6% interest from Borrow Section in VP
BorrowerC select a project type from Catalogue and develops the ProjectD. When ProjectD is finished he returns Loan+Interest to VP Borrow Section. InvestorB can take back his investment+interest from InvestorA Wallet. BorrowerC distributes keeps 40% of profit and distribute 40% to InvestorA and 20% to Co-Serving platform which managed and coded all transactions.The property of ProjectD remains in InvestorA Wallet till all the smartcontracts conditions are sucessfully resolved
